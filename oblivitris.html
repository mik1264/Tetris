How Memory Mechanics Work

  • Gradual Fade: Placed pieces are fully visible for 3 seconds, then fade to 50% opacity, then disappear completely after 10 seconds
  • Sonar Pulse: Press S or click button to briefly reveal all hidden blocks (3 pulses per game)
  • Memory Strain: The longer you survive, the faster pieces fade - your memory is tested!
  • Ghost Memory: Your last 5 placed pieces show as faint outlines to help track recent moves
  • Penalty: Trying to place a piece in an occupied space (that you forgot about) costs you a life
  • Archaeologist Mode: Clear lines without using sonar pulses for bonus points - prove perfect memory!
