What You've Learned:

Forms of Life: Language is embedded in different social practices, each with its own rules and conventions. A word in one form of life may function completely differently in another.


Miscommunication: Many misunderstandings arise not from ignorance, but from assuming everyone is playing the same language game when they're actually in different forms of life.


Building Bridges: Understanding requires recognizing which language game someone is playing and translating between different practices.


No Universal Language: There's no "pure" language above all forms of life. We must always work within and between specific practices.
