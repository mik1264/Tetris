Master Three Timelines Simultaneously

In Chronotris, you control three parallel timelines at once!


The Timelines:

  • PAST: What has happened - affects the present
  • PRESENT: Your main timeline - what is happening now
  • FUTURE: Ghost preview of what will happen

Temporal Mechanics:

  • Clearing lines in PAST causes cascade effects in PRESENT
  • FUTURE shows 3 pieces ahead as ghosts
  • Press SPACE to merge all timelines for 10 seconds!
  • Create paradoxes at your own risk...
