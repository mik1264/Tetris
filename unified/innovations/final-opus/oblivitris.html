The Ultimate Memory Challenge:

Welcome to Oblivitris, where placed pieces gradually fade from visibility.


The Fade Mechanism:

  • Pieces are fully visible for 3 seconds after placement
  • Then fade to 50% opacity over the next 3 seconds
  • After 10 seconds, they disappear completely
  • You must remember where everything is!

Your Tools:

  • Sonar Pulse (Q key): Briefly reveals all hidden blocks (3 uses per game)
  • Ghost Memory: Your last 5 placed pieces show as faint outlines
  • Memory Bonus: Extra points for clearing invisible lines
Difficulty Progression: The longer you survive, the faster pieces fade. Train your spatial memory and become an Oblivitris master!
