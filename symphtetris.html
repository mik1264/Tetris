How Music Works

  • 7 Instruments: Each tetromino is a different instrument (I=Bass, O=Drums, T=Piano, S=Violin, Z=Trumpet, J=Sax, L=Synth)
  • Vertical Pitch: Higher placement = higher musical pitch; lower placement = lower pitch
  • Horizontal Timing: Left side = early in measure; right side = later in measure
  • Chord Clearing: Clearing a line with harmonically compatible pieces sounds pleasant and gives bonus points
  • Dissonance Penalty: Bad note combinations create harsh sounds and reduce points
  • Genre Modes:
    • Classical: Rewards structured patterns and harmony
    • Jazz: Rewards improvisation and variety
    • Electronic: Rewards repetition and rhythm
